    overview

    Tekken is a popular fighting game series developed and published by Namco.The first entry in the Tekken series was originally released for arcades in 1994, and ported to the playStation the following year.

    The gameplay allows players to control each of the fighter's four limbs independently. The series later introduced combos and special moves, with characters also able to stage break arenas.

    The original arcade version features eight playable fighters. Each has a special "sub-boss" associated with them, who will be fought in Stage 8, followed by the final boss, Heihachi Mishima.

    The first entry in the Tekken series was followed by a sequel, Tekken 2, released in 1995. The latest entry in the series is Tekken 8, which introduces an all-new game mechanic called the ”Heat System,” giving players the ability to harness aggressiveness as their weapon, and incorporate these new offensive tactics into their playstyle.
